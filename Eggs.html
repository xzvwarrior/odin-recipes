How to cook eggs

You will need the following ingredients to cook eggs:

  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

First you should crack your egg open into a bowl, making sure to not have any egg shell shards getting into your bowl.

Spinkle in some salt and pepper into the eggs and then take a fork and whisk the eggs until they're completely homogenous,

Place butter or oil into pain at a medium heat and allow it to get to temperature before pouring your egg mixture into the pan.

Allow for the eggs to cook for about 2 minutes or until the egg has began to "Set"(where it's no longer as watery) and then flip the eggs over using a spatula.

When this side is cooked through add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste and plate it.
